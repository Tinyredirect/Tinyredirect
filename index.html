Your Tip Link

Why You Should Use TinyRedirect

TinyRedirect allows you to easily share your cryptocurrency address with others. With a personalized tip link and a unique QR code, you can make tipping and receiving crypto quick and easy.

How to Use

  • Click "Connect Wallet" to connect your MetaMask wallet.
  • Once connected, your wallet address will appear and a personalized tip link will be generated.
  • Share the tip link with others and receive cryptocurrency tips directly to your wallet.

Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. We do not store any personal information or the funds you receive. Your wallet address is only used to generate a unique tip link.
